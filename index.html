About Us

Ygnd El-Ras is the first Emirati tea brand specialized in providing the finest black tea blends. Our tea leaves are imported from different countries in the world.
Ygnd El-Ras offers five different kinds of black tea blends that comprise of: 9 blends, saffron black tea, rose black tea, cardamom black tea and karak blend.

Our Blends

Our Vision

To be one of the world’s leading brands of tea blends in the UAE.

Our Mission

To provide high quality tea blends that combine Arabian, Indian and European taste for our consumers.

We are unique because we mix the different aromas of tea from around the globe and we bring it in a single blend of tea which combines the true essence of Arabian, European and Indian taste.

Our Values

To preserve the family legacy in order to become a successful business.

To achieve heights of sustainability through our activities with sustainable and social awareness throughout our involvement in practices that diminishes our negative environmental impact and increases the positive impact on society.

To bring Emirati cultural traditions of tea under the light of modernization.

To support today’s generation and give them the opportunity to develop their skills through our brand in various fields.
